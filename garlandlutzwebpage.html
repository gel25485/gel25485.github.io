Introduction

My name is Garland Elaine Lutz. I was born on September 15, 1998, to Eric and Stacey Lutz. I was born in Valdosta, Georgia. My family lived in Homerville until soon after my brother was born. We then moved to the family farm, where my father and grandfather put in a blackberry field. My father owns the independent insurance agency in Homerville, and my mother is the in charge of the Governor's Leadership Academy. The blackberries did not last long, and the fields were soon converted to blueberries. My parents divorced when I was ten, and my dad, brother, and myself have lived on the farm since.

I attended Clinch County High School eigth through twelvth grade. I was involved with Senior Beta throughout high school. I was a four-year varsity starter for softball and tennis. In softball and tennis, I moved positions each year. My senior year, I was the lead defender on my high school's mock trial team. My tennis team made it to the elite eight.
